Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our truck and trailer repair services

Do you provide emergency roadside service?

Yes, we offer 24/7 emergency roadside service. We have multiple service trucks ready to respond to your location at any time, day or night. There is an additional call-out fee for services outside regular business hours.

What areas do you service?

We are based in Fontana, CA and Russellville, AR, but we travel to provide service wherever needed. No matter the distance, if we can come to an agreement, we are willing to travel to assist you.

Do you carry parts on your service trucks?

Yes, our trucks are fully stocked with common parts including brake chambers, air valves, leveling valves, air bags, coolant hoses, starters, alternators, batteries, and more. We operate as a "Shop on Wheels" to get you back on the road quickly.

What types of vehicles do you service?

We specialize in heavy-duty trucks and trailers. Our certified mechanics have experience with commercial vehicles and are equipped with specialized tools and equipment for these types of repairs.

Do you offer towing services?

Yes, we provide towing services as part of our comprehensive roadside assistance. Our goal is to get you back on the road no matter what, whether through on-site repair or towing to a service facility.

How quickly can you respond to a service call?

Response times vary depending on location and current call volume, but we strive to respond as quickly as possible. Our multiple service trucks and 24/7 availability help ensure rapid response to your needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our truck and trailer repair services

Do you provide 24/7 emergency service?

Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency roadside service. Regular hours are Monday-Friday 8am-6pm and Saturday 8am-12pm. All other hours have an additional call-out fee.

What areas do you service?

We service Fontana, California and Russellville, Arkansas areas. We provide mobile service and can come to your location.

Do you work on all types of trucks and trailers?

Yes, we service all makes and models of trucks and trailers. Our experienced technicians can handle everything from light-duty trucks to heavy-duty commercial vehicles.

How quickly can you respond to an emergency call?

We typically respond to emergency calls within 30-60 minutes depending on your location and current service load. We prioritize safety and get you back on the road as quickly as possible.
